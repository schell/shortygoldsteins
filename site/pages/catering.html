Catering

Let Shorty Goldstein’s bring hand-made Jewish Deli food to your next event. We are available for catering of all sizes. For parties up to 100 people, please allow 72 hours lead time. For larger events, and certain items, more notice may be necessary, as everything made by hand, in house.

Delivery is available in the Bay Area. Delivery fees are not included in catering menu pricing.

Compostable plates, napkins and utensils available upon request.
